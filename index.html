The Geography of Guilt

User Guide

This website can be explored in two ways: as a guided story and as a map.

  • Click Start to enter the narrative slideshow from the beginning.
  • Use Next and Back to move through Raskolnikov’s journey.
  • Each slide shows the location image first; use the media gallery beneath it to open additional images or video.
  • Open Read Quote & Analysis to expand the literary evidence and interpretation for that scene.
  • After the story, return to the map and click any numbered red location dot to jump directly into that part of the narrative.
  • The numbered labels on the map follow the order of the slideshow.
